What is a Rage Room? A rage room (also known as a smash room or anger room) is a space where people can vent their anger by destroying objects. It's a safe, controlled environment where you can let loose and break things without consequences. Our Story Founded in 2020, our rage room started with a simple mission: provide a fun and therapeutic way for people to release stress and anger. Since then, we've helped thousands of people smash away their frustrations. Safety First All participants receive proper safety equipment including helmets, gloves, and protective clothing. Our staff is trained to ensure your experience is both fun and safe.
